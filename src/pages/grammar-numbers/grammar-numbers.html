When learning Filipino names of numbers, it is also useful to learn the Spanish ones as well (spelled according to the conventions of Tagalog phonetics) since these are still commonly used, especially among the older generations. So in this lesson, we give the Spanish equivalents of numbers if they are commonly used by Filipinos.

Ordinal Numbers

An ordinal number indicates the place occupied by an item in an ordered sequence. Filipino ordinals are easy to put together and remember. The prefix ika- or pang- is added to the cardinal numbers.
